Volunteer Opportunities

We need your help to make each event a success! Whether you're assisting on race day or helping behind the scenes, volunteering is a great way to support the running community.

Event Day Volunteers

Role: Assist with race day logistics, registration, water stations, cheering, and finish line support.

Perks: Volunteer t-shirt, free race entry for a future event, and a chance to meet amazing runners!

Course Marshals

Role: Guide runners along the course, cheer them on, and ensure safety.

Perks: Front-row seat to the race action and a volunteer gift bag.

Race Packet Pickup Volunteers

Role: Help distribute race packets and bib numbers before the race.

Perks: A sneak peek at the event and meeting fellow runners!

Post-Race Volunteers

Role: Assist with post-race activities, handing out medals, serving refreshments, and supporting the awards ceremony.

Perks: Celebrate with the runners and enjoy the post-race festivities!
