Become a Sole Mate

Joining Sole Mates is easy! Whether you're new to running or a seasoned athlete, we welcome all levels. Our community thrives on support, motivation, and fun!

As a member, you'll get exclusive access to weekly group runs, race discounts, and so much more!

Membership Benefits

  • Weekly Group Runs: Join us for fun, social runs every week with members of all skill levels.
  • Race Discounts: Save on entry fees for races throughout the year.
  • Exclusive Events: Access to members-only events and races.
  • Supportive Community: Meet new friends and fellow runners who will encourage and motivate you every step of the way!
