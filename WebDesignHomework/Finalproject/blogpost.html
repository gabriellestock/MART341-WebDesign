How to Perfect Your Winged Eyeliner

Are you tired of messing up your entire makeup look just because of one step? One step in the whole routine that can either absolutely make or break your entire makeup look. Would you beleive me if I told you its way easier than it looks? Probably not because I am just some random girl obsessed with makeup. But, I think you should trust me.

The phrase I say is, "The best way to get the best eyeliner is to not use it at all! That may sound confusing, but its true. The only way I can ever achieve my perfect wing is when I do not use eyeliner. Now, that may be because I'm not a makeup artist and eyeliner stresses me out because its typically really hard to erase and there is little room for forgiveness. But, this is exactly why I am sitting here writing this blog. I am not here to give you unrealistic tips that only a makeup artist could complete. I am here to give you realistic tips that you can incorporate into your daily makeup routine.

Now, if I don't use eyeliner, then what do I use you may ask? Eyeshadow. A product that is very forgiving but also lasts!
