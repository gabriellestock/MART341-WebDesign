About Skincare

Skincare is essential for maintaining healthy and glowing skin. In this blog, I will share tips, product reviews, and my personal skincare journey. As well as some makeup tutorials and my favorite products sprinkled in, for when the skin needs a little cover-up.

My Personal Skincare Routine

Here is my daily morning skincare routine:

  • Cleanser: I use a gentle cleanser in the morning, depending on your skin, if you have dry skin for exampl, you might want your cleanser to be oil based and vice versa.
  • Toner: I love to use Paula's Choice toner with a cotton pad right after I wash my face. This helps to balance my skin's pH.
  • Moisturizer: Keeping my skin hydrated is crucial. So I use a combination of moisterizers. I really like CereVe's Moisturizing Cream.
  • Sunscreen: Protecting my skin from UV rays is a must! I really like my Elemis collagen sunscreen, but it is pricy. Any facial sunscreen works, as long as you use it!
