Hardworking and detail-oriented marketing professional with a strong academic record, consistently earning a spot on the President's List since my freshman year of college. Through these experiences, I showcased my expertise in content marketing, refined my ability to develop strategic initiatives, and built valuable professional connections.

With expertise in digital design, content creation, and team collaboration, I bring a meticulous approach to every project. My academic achievements and practical experiences reflect my commitment to excellence, adaptability, and a passion for delivering high-quality results in the marketing field.
