Website Idea

Personal Skincare & Beauty Blog

For my personal blog website I would create a vibrant skincare and beauty blog where readers can explore skincare routines, product reviews, and makeup tutorials. I would create a colorful and fun design with features such as tips for better skincare and makeup. I would want to incorporate videos, quirky write-ups and humor to make skincare and beauty fun!

College tips blog

I would create a website that offers guidance on everything from study strategies and time management to navigating campus life and career planning. I would include my personal college sucess stories as well as my fellow students. It would be a a reliable support system for students looking to excel academically and personally. I would shoot for a more professional tone on this website.

Favorite Books journal

For this website idea, I would create a website highlighting all the books I have read. I would describe each book, why I liked it, and how I rated it. I would create a section on the website for people to respond to my reviews and allow them to also share hoe they're thoughts and feelings about the books. I would want the tone of this website to be warm and inviting for those who like to read.
